About StudentBuddy AI

StudentBuddy AI is an AI-powered assistant designed to help university students manage their academic life more efficiently. Our platform combines an intelligent AI assistant with productivity tools such as reminders, task management, and student FAQs. The goal of StudentBuddy AI is to reduce stress, improve productivity, and help students stay organized throughout their university journey.
